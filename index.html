Welcome

Flipping one page at a time

Hello, I am Rishav, an EC engineer from Heritage Institute of Technology, Kolkata (HITK) batch of 2020. I am fascinated by the world of Electronics and have developed a keen interest in Robotics, IoT, and Drones.

About Me

I’ve worked as a Research Assistant in Jadavpur University, Kolkata, on a project regarding Electronic Nose for Cardamom detection, supported by the Govt. of West Bengal. I have served as a senior member of the IEEE Student Branch of my college and have been involved in several social, and educational and technical events conducted by our Student Branch. Have led the Aeronautics Club of HITK, as a Senior Coordinatior. Under my leadership, various technical workshops were organised.
My Motto: To inspire and get inspired, Always!
